What is Emotional Intelligence?

The ability to understand emotions - both yours and others. Here I'd say it's mostly about recognising when you might be feeling overwhelmed or frustrated and being able to take a moment and respond with intention. I think it's also about being aware of how those around you might be feeling and being sensitive to that.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is a way of measuring how 'intelligent' someone is in a technical sense (book smart).

EQ is a way of measuring how 'intelligent' someone is in an emotional sense (people smart).

Why is EQ important?

I think EQ is the difference between doing well in this course and not. You could be super smart (book smart) and get through the content alright but without emotional intelligence, you might struggle with group work (a big part of the course) or with recognising and responding to your own or other people’s emotions.

Background

Te Whare Tapa Whā is a wellbeing model that offers a Māori perspective on health - focusing on four main parts:

  • Taha Tinana - Physical Wellbeing
  • Taha Hinengaro - Mental Wellbeing
  • Taha Wairua - Spiritual Wellbeing
  • Taha Whānau - Family Wellbeing

The model is often represented by a wharenui (meeting house), where each of the four walls represent one of these parts. They're all connected to the whenua (land, sea and natural world) which forms the foundation for overall wellbeing.
